Castling Capital Group - Private Equity & Management

Access the highest-quality real estate investments

Castling Capital acquires, invests and manages real estate assets with a focus on both near-term income generation and long-term value creation.

Pinchus Scwhartz, Founder and CEO, created Castling Capital to provide everyday investors access to the institutional-grade real estate based opportunities that are normally reserved for only the largest investors.

About Us

Castling Capital Group is the industry’s preeminent boutique real estate capital advisory firm.

Castling offers a wide variety of cutting-edge commercial real estate capital markets financing solutions, including debt and mezzanine financing, preferred and joint venture equity, sponsor equity, and investment sales. The privately held company is headquartered in New York City with, The firm is highly regarded in the industry for its integrity, creativity, and client-advocacy. Our distinctive advisory services are tailored to service LP and GP investors, family offices and high net individuals, institutional and private funds.

Our approach

What We Do

For the last 10 years Castling advised experienced real estate and finance professionals and acquired a sizeable real estate portfolio, By collaboratively working with our partners, we were able to be a big part in growth of many now well known real estate enterprises both on the finance side and investor side, now we are here to take all our relationships and experiences we have built to level the playing field and guide everyday people how to build and preserve wealth. Castling selects investment opportunities ranging from residential real estate and development, Commercial and industrial assets, healthcare, hospitality, and related businesses among others.

Aligning experience with equity

Castling has leveraged their robust deal flow for direct investments as well as buyout and growth equity co-investments since 2016.  By aligning our expertise in Real Estate Capital to our portfolio , Castling maximizes our capabilities to help investors scale, and navigate markets in a capital efficient manner.
